About Me

I am a 2nd year Ph.D. student at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Yixuan Yuan. I received my M.Eng from Shanghai Jiao Tong University under Prof. Jie Yang and Prof. Yun Gu. I obtained my B.Eng. in Automation at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

Previously, I was dedicated to advancing 3D/4D healthcare, including human body regions such as chest, abdomen, and oral cavity, as well as data modalities like CT, X-ray, and point clouds.

My current research focuses on achieving Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) by developing integrated systems that leverage Multimodal Large Language Models for advanced visual understanding and generation.
